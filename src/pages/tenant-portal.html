Subtitle

Collect payments – online and or offline

Residents can pay fees or rent online, view their payment history, and set up recurring payments. The resident portal is available via phone, tablet, or desktop, so there are no more excuses for late rent or association fees.

Subtitle

Streamline maintenance requests

Residents can submit maintenance requests and upload photos directly from their phone or tablet. They can see when an item’s been assigned to a staff member or vendor, and view status updates until the item is completed, so they won’t need to call to check in.

Subtitle

Text and email important updates

Share important notices and updates with residents using the announcements board, draft and send emails to individuals or groups, or send text messages to residents or association owners.

Subtitle

Build a community

Add a community message board to the portal, so residents and associations can share and discuss news and updates. It allows conversations to continue even after board meetings have ended, so it’s great for associations, too.

Subtitle

Store and share important documents

Residents can download their lease or other important documents (trash reminders, addendums) from their portal, so they won’t need to call and ask for a copy of them.

Subtitle

Look professional and take control

Take control of your brand and make the portal your own. With one click, upload your logo and we’ll find the perfect colors to match! Decide which pages you want to share with residents, owners and even board members. The choices are entirely in your hands.
