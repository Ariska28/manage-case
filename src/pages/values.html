Our Values

Our Values guide all aspects of how we run our company. If you know anyone who shares them and might be interested in joining us, we are hiring.

Ownership mentality

Every team member has the power to to make our company better.

01
Don’t optimize for the short term

Short-term benefits never justify long-term sacrifices. Build for the future.

02
We are builders

We are building collectively the product and our company.

03
Go the extra mile

Go beyond delivering what just works. Deliver what excites our customers.

04
Do what’s right

What is right isn’t the same as what’s easy.

05
Be transparent

Share information. Share errors and mistakes. Share your successes.
