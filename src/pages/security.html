Security at ManageCasa

Security is one of the top priorities at ManageCasa. We’re committed to maintaining the trust of our clients.. Here are some of the ways we protect your information and data:
  • We don’t share any information with 3rd party organizations for their own direct marketing puposes.
  • We use 256-bit AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) to protect the transmission of your data to our application.
  • We have on-site security engineers, that investigate and resolve to security threads as quickly as possible.
  • Independent third parties also assess our site for potential issues.
  • We host all data on AWS, the world’s leading and most secure cloud capacity service provider that helps us with physical, procedural, and technical steps to safeguard our application as well as our storage environment, to protect your personal information to comply with federal privacy and security standards.
